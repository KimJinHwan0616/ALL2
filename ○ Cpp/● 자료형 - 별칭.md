## 자료형 
>`using 별칭 = 자료형`: 템플릿O
>```
>using size_t = unsinged int;
>using uint32_t = unsinged int;
>using uint64_t = unsinged long long;
>``` 
>
>`typedef 자료형 별칭`: 템플릿X
>```
>typedef T Type;
>
>typedef unsinged int uint32_t;
>typedef unsinged int size_t;
>typedef unsinged long long uint64_t;
>```
